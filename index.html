About Us

A Family Business Built on Affordable, Sustainable Living

We are a small, family-run business providing purpose-built eco accommodation in North Edinburgh — affordable homes for students and young professionals, with no stress of managing separate utility bills.

Founded by Robert MacKenzie, a qualified building surveyor with over 20 years of Edinburgh property management experience, our flats were designed on Passivhaus principles: lower energy use, cleaner air, and a smaller carbon footprint.

Sustainability

Built Green from the Ground Up

Designed on Passivhaus principles — reducing your carbon footprint and keeping energy costs down.

🪟

Triple Glazing

State-of-the-art triple-glazed windows lock heat in and keep noise out, reducing heating costs year-round.

☀️

Rooftop Solar Panels

Extensive solar panel coverage on the roof greatly reduces the building's carbon emissions and electricity costs.

🌀

Heat Recovery System

A ventilation system with air filter recovers heat from outgoing air and supplies fresh, filtered air throughout.

🏠

High Insulation

Highly insulated walls and roof retain warmth efficiently, keeping your home comfortable in all seasons.
